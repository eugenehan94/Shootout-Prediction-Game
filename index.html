Soccer Shoot-Out Prediction Game Scoreboard Instructions

Click one of the six quadrant in the net area to select the area you would block. The shooter (computer) will select a random quadrant and if it matches your selection, then you have blocked his/her shot, else it would be a goal.

The game doesn't stop, but when either the goals and blocks on the scoreboard reaches 100, it will restart at zero.
